About Me

Jude Fernando BScEng, MEng, CPEng, NER, MIEAust, MAMC, CAMA (Certified Asset Management Assessor for ISO 55001)

Founder and Principal

I am Jude, founder and principal of JUFANDO.

Over 17 years of multi-disciplinary professional experience I have supported businesses improving their people, processes and capital productivity through Research & Development, Mechanical Engineering Systems, Reliability Engineering, Strategic Asset Management, Maintenance Management and Lean Operations and Manufacturing.

I have gained my experience while working in metals, power, FMCG, food, light manufacturing and infrastructure industries in Australia, Asia and Middle East.

After starting my career journey as a junior engineer, I grew to reach plant/site manager roles. During this journey I have served in design, mechatronics, production, manufacturing, maintenance, reliability, asset management and lean professional roles while standing for overall business management responsibilities.
